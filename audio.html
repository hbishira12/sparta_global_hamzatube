panther growling

When panthers do make sounds they will usually chirp, peep, whistle, purr, moan, scream, growl or hiss. Females signal their readiness to mate by yowling or caterwauling. The Florida Panther is one of the most critically endangered animals on the planet.

Lion hissing and snorting

Big cats -- lions, tigers, jaguars, and leopards -- cannot purr. However, they can snort and hiss, something a domestic cat is able to do just imagine In fact, no cat can both roar and meow.

lion roaring

not all cats roar (which is probably a good thing for those of us who own housecats), but those that do fascinate us with their mysterious and frightening sounds. Research published gives us new insight into the inner workings of the roars of lions and tigers.
